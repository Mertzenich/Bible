<a id="orgb42375f"></a>

## Proverbs 31:2

<a id="org4fc4d30"></a>

## Proverbs 31:3

<a id="org68f5125"></a>

## Proverbs 31:4

<a id="org86a34b4"></a>

## Proverbs 31:5

<a id="org4824ba6"></a>

## Proverbs 31:6

<a id="orgb8ef62c"></a>

## Proverbs 31:7

<a id="org1c3a31a"></a>
